About the project

A lightweight recommendation prototype for cultural content in a target language.

Goal & MVP

Goal

Suggest music, films/series and videos aligned with the learner‚Äôs preferences in their target language, to make practice feel natural and enjoyable.

MVP scope

Start with one content type (e.g. films) and one target language. Use genre similarity and popularity to retrieve a Top 5 of suggestions.

The emphasis is on validating the experience before scaling content types and languages.

Two input modes

  • Filters Quick, declarative preferences entered via language, type and genres.
  • History Optional upload for finer personalisation when a history source becomes available.
